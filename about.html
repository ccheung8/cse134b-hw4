About me

My name is Calvin Cheung.

UX Designer, Web Developer, Gym Rat, Snowboarder, and Anime Enjoyer. As a designer, I strive to create experiences that are not only usable and accessible, but enjoyable.

I strive in fast paced environments where I get to contribute to a variety of different projects. Being driven and a quick learner, I always put my best foot forward and tend to get lost in my work.

Having had the privilege to work with a diverse cast of people on a variety of different projects, I've been able to immerse myself in different user groups and practice empathy wherever I end up.

Outside of designing and developing, I love making people laugh, watching TV shows, and long walks on the beach.
